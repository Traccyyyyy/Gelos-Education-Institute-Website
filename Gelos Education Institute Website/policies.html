Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to TipToe _Soles. We are committed to protecting your privacy and providing a safe online shopping experience. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, and protection of your personal information.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information when you visit our website:

  • Personal Information: This may include your name, email address, shipping address, billing address, phone number, and payment information.
  • Browsing Information: We collect data about your device, IP address, browser type, and operating system.
  • Usage Information: We may collect information about your interactions with our website, such as pages visited, products viewed, and the time and date of your visit.
  • Cookies: We use cookies to enhance your experience. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Order Processing: To process and fulfill your orders, including payment processing and shipping.
  • Customer Support: To respond to your inquiries, provide assistance, and address your requests.
  • Marketing: To send you promotional materials and updates about our products and services if you have opted in to receive them.
  • Analytics: To analyze website usage and improve our website's performance and user experience.

4. Sharing Your Information

We may share your information with third parties for specific purposes, including:

  • Payment processors for order processing.
  • Shipping carriers to deliver your orders.
  • Marketing and advertising partners.
  • Legal and regulatory authorities when required by law.

5. Security

We take reasonable measures to safeguard your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration.

6. Your Choices

You have certain rights and choices regarding your personal information:

  • You can opt out of marketing communications.
  • You can access, update, or delete your personal information by contacting us.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as needed. We will notify you of any significant changes via email or by posting a notice on our website.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us .
