FAQ

How can I apply for a course at Gelos?

To apply for a course at Gelos, visit our website, select your desired program, and complete the online application form. Ensure you meet the admission requirements for your chosen course.

What is the cost of tuition and fees for various courses?

Tuition fees vary depending on the program. Please refer to our website or contact our admissions office for specific information on course fees and any available financial aid options.

What academic support services are available?

Gelos offers a range of academic support services, including tutoring, study groups, and counseling. Our Student Services team is here to help you succeed.

Are there flexible study options like online courses or part-time programs?

Yes, Gelos offers flexible study options, including online courses and part-time programs, to accommodate your schedule and learning preferences.

What facilities and resources are available to students on campus?

Gelos campuses offer state-of-the-art facilities, including libraries, labs, and recreational areas. Students have access to Wi-Fi, computer labs, and study spaces.
